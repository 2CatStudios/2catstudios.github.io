NewTown

Work in Progress

New Town will be a life simulator, similar to the 1998 Nintendo game "Animal Crossing". It will feature many of the same core mechanics and concepts that were in the original Nintendo game, but at the same time, it will bring many new ideas to this entertaining genre. Please keep an eye on this page for future updates. More information coming soon!
